# Behind The PowerPoint — L&D Portfolio

A selection of interactive learning tools built by **Behind The PowerPoint**, an independent L&D design consultancy specialising in contact centre and sales enablement environments. These tools reflect a design philosophy centred on preparing people, not just training them — built from the frontline up.

---

## Tools in this portfolio

### 01 — Call Readiness Platform
`AI-powered` `Voice-based` `Financial services`

A voice-based sales training platform for financial services advisors. Advisors work through each stage of a structured sales conversation using their voice, with AI analysis of compliance, delivery, and tone. Built around BCOBS regulatory requirements. Includes a manager review portal for sign-off and oversight.

[View live tool →](https://btp-createyourscript.netlify.app)

---

### 02 — AI Conversation Simulator
`AI-powered` `Voice-based` `White-label demo`

A full agent readiness platform combining a structured knowledge assessment with live voice call simulations. Advisors respond to realistic customer scenarios using their voice; AI assesses every turn in real time with per-turn coaching feedback and an overall readiness score. Fully white-labelled and customisable.

[View live tool →](https://simulatordemo.netlify.app)

---

### 03 — MyCoach Self-Review
`Self-coaching` `Behaviour change`

A guided self-coaching tool for contact centre advisors. Walks advisors through a structured six-step reflection on a call of their choice — from mindset-setting through to a single committed behaviour change. Features a strength-before-development unlock mechanic, designed to build self-awareness and ownership of performance.

[View live tool →](https://btp-selfcoaching.netlify.app)

## About Behind The PowerPoint

Behind The PowerPoint is an independent L&D design consultancy focused on contact centres and scaling sales environments. Our tools are built with a frontline-first philosophy — starting with what advisors actually experience on a call, and designing backwards from there. We build things that prepare people, not just inform them.
